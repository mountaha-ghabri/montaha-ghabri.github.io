get to know me

I am a data scientist and applied econometrician specializing in causal inference, time-series forecasting, and policy evaluation. My work bridges rigorous statistical methodology with real-world decision-making — from designing staggered difference-in-differences estimators and Bayesian VAR models to building production A/B testing pipelines with CUPED variance reduction. I hold advanced training in modern econometric techniques (J-PAL, DiD/RDD, panel methods) and enjoy translating complex evidence into actionable insights for teams and stakeholders.
